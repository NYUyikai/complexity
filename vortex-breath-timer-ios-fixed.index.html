How to practice

  1. Sit with spine upright, eyes closed. Breathe through your nose throughout.
  2. Follow the descending Fibonacci pattern: 13s → 8s → 5s → 3s → 2s → 1s, both inhale and exhale at each level.
  3. Let the breath become naturally shallower as intervals shorten — do not force full breaths on short counts.
  4. At the still point, the 432 Hz singing bowl tone sounds. Rest here as long as feels natural — the timer auto-advances after 13 seconds.
  5. The sound layers shift with each phase: wide spiral music for the long breaths, formless texture for compression, pure tone for the still point.
